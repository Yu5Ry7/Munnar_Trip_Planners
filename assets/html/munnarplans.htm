Day 1

Arrive in Munnar and check into your accommodation.

Start your day with a visit to the Tea Gardens of Munnar. Enjoy the scenic beauty and learn about the tea-making process.

Explore the Echo Point, known for its natural echo phenomenon. Shout out and listen to your voice reverberate!

Head to the Kundala Lake and enjoy a leisurely boat ride amidst the serene surroundings.

End the day by witnessing a mesmerizing sunset at the Top Station viewpoint.

Return to your accommodation and relax for the night.

Day 2

Start the day early to Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr. Enjoy a guided trekking experience in the park.

Proceed to the Tea Museum, where you can learn about the history and production of tea in Munnar through interactive exhibits.

Visit the scenic Pothamedu Viewpoint and capture panoramic views of the tea gardens and mist-covered hills. Explore the aromatic spice plantations in the area and learn about the cultivation of various spices.

In the evening, visit the Attukad Waterfalls and enjoy the cascading beauty of the waterfalls.

Return to your accommodation and relax for the night.

Note: The Eravikulam National Park tickets can be booked in advance or at the counter. The park is closed from Feb to April due to the breeding time of goats. Tea Museum is closed on Monday.

Day 3

Embark on an early morning sunrise trek to Meesapulimala, the second-highest peak in the Western Ghats. Enjoy breathtaking views of the sunrise and surrounding landscapes.

Next, visit the picturesque Anamudi Peak, the highest peak in South India.

Enjoy a trek to the summit or explore the surrounding trails for a closer encounter with nature.

Afterward, head to the picturesque Lakkam Waterfalls and spend some time relaxing amidst the serene ambiance and cascading water.

Visit the Chinnakanal Waterfalls, another stunning waterfall nestled in the lush greenery. Take in the beauty of the falls and capture some memorable photographs.

In the evening, indulge in some shopping and pick up local specialties such as tea, spices, and handmade chocolates.

Bid farewell to Munnar and depart for your onward journey
