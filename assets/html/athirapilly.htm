Day 1

Pick up from Munnar and drive to Athirapally (approximately 135 km, 4 hours).

Check-in at a hotel or resort in Athirapally.

Visit the magnificent Athirapally Waterfalls, one of the largest and most beautiful waterfalls in Kerala. Enjoy the breathtaking views and spend some time amidst nature.

Explore the surrounding lush greenery and take a walk along the trails near the waterfalls.

In the evening, you can relax by the riverside or visit the nearby Vazhachal Waterfalls.

Overnight stay in Athirapally.

Day 2

After breakfast, visit the nearby Charpa Waterfalls, which is known for its scenic beauty and tranquil surroundings.

Explore the nearby Anakkayam and Malakkappara areas, known for their tea and spice plantations. Enjoy the aroma of fresh tea leaves and spices.

You can also opt for a thrilling jungle safari in the nearby Sholayar Forest Range, where you can spot various wildlife species and enjoy the natural beauty of the region.

In the evening, you can go for a leisurely boat ride on the Chalakudy River and enjoy the serene atmosphere.

Overnight stay in Athirapally.

Day 3

After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and proceed to visit the nearby attractions:

Visit the picturesque Thumboormuzhi Dam and Butterfly Garden, where you can witness the scenic beauty, explore the garden, and learn about different species of butterflies.

Explore the nearby Ezhattumugham Hanging Bridge and enjoy the panoramic views of the surrounding forests and river.

In the afternoon, start your return journey to Munnar.

Drop-off at the desired location in Munnar.
