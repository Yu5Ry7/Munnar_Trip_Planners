Day 1

Pick up from Munnar and drive to Vagamon (approximately 65 km, 2 hours).

Check-in at a hotel or resort in Vagamon.

Visit the famous Vagamon Pine Forest, known for its serene ambiance and tall pine trees. Enjoy a peaceful walk through the forest and take in the fresh mountain air.

Explore the Vagamon Meadows, vast green meadows surrounded by hills. You can have a picnic, indulge in some outdoor activities, or simply relax amidst nature.

In the evening, visit the Thangal Para, a sacred site for Muslims. It offers a panoramic view of the surrounding hills and is known for its religious significance.

Overnight stay in Vagamon.

Day 2

After breakfast, visit the beautiful Vagamon Lake, also known as the Vagamon Reservoir. Enjoy boating or simply relax by the lakeside, surrounded by picturesque views.

Explore the Kurisumala Ashram, a serene Christian pilgrimage center located on a hill. Take a peaceful walk up the hill and enjoy the serene atmosphere and panoramic views.

Visit the Murugan Mala, a hill dedicated to Lord Murugan. Climb the steps to reach the hilltop temple and enjoy the panoramic views of the surroundings.

In the evening, you can visit the Suicide Point, which offers a breathtaking view of the valley and is known for its scenic beauty.

Overnight stay in Vagamon.

Day 3

After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and proceed to visit the following attractions:

Visit the Pine Valley, a dense pine forest known for its natural beauty. Take a leisurely walk through the forest and enjoy the tranquility.

Explore the beautiful Tea Gardens of Vagamon and learn about the tea production process. Enjoy the scenic beauty of the tea plantations and capture some memorable photographs.

In the afternoon, start your return journey to Munnar.

Drop-off at the desired location in Munnar.
