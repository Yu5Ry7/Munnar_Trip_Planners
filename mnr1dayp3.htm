Day 1

•Lockhart View Point: Marvel at the stunning views from Lockhart View Point and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the surroundings.

•Tea Museum: Explore the Tea Museum and learn about the history and process of tea production in Munnar.

•Eravikulam National Park: Visit Eravikulam National Park, home to endangered Nilgiri Tahr and enjoy the breathtaking views of the rolling hills and tea plantations.

•Thalayaar View Point: Capture panoramic views of the majestic Thalayaar Waterfall and the surrounding landscape.

•Lakkom Waterfall: Witness the beauty of Lakkom Waterfall, cascading down amidst lush greenery and rocky cliffs.

•Drop at Munnar: Conclude the day by dropping you off at your accommodation in Munnar.
