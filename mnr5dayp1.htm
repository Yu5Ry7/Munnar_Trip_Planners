Day 1

•Thalayaar View Point: Enjoy panoramic views from Thalayaar View Point and marvel at the beauty of the surrounding landscapes.

•Lakkom Waterfall: Visit Lakkom Waterfall and witness the cascading waters amidst the serene natural setting.

•Marayoor Sandalwood Reserve: Explore the Marayoor Sandalwood Reserve and learn about the cultivation and significance of sandalwood in the region.

•Munnar: Return to Munnar and spend some time exploring the charming town.

•Kanthaloor Apple Farm: Visit the picturesque Kanthaloor Apple Farm and immerse yourself in the scenic beauty of the apple orchards.

•Munnar: Return to Munnar and enjoy more of its attractions and natural beauty.

•Erachillpara Waterfalls (Seasonal): If in season, visit the stunning Erachillpara Waterfalls and witness the captivating beauty of the cascades.

•Check Dam (Seasonal): If in season, explore the Check Dam and enjoy the serene surroundings and views.

•Shooting Point: Capture memorable photographs at Shooting Point, known for its panoramic views and picturesque landscapes.

•Strawberry Farm: Visit a strawberry farm and indulge in the delight of plucking and tasting fresh strawberries.

•Honeyrock: Explore Honeyrock, known for its honey production, and learn about the process of beekeeping and honey extraction.

•Jaggery Factory: Visit a jaggery factory and gain insights into the traditional process of making jaggery, a sweet delicacy.

Day 2

•Signal Point View:Enjoy the panoramic views from Signal Point and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the surroundings.

•Flower Garden:Explore the vibrant Flower Garden and marvel at the colorful blooms and intricate designs.

•Photo Point:Capture beautiful memories at Photo Point, known for its picturesque backdrops and stunning landscapes.

•Elephant Park:Interact with majestic elephants and witness their incredible presence at the Elephant Park.

•Botanical Garden:Discover the rich flora and fauna at the Botanical Garden, home to a diverse range of plant species.

•Mattupatty Dam: The scenic Mattupatty Dam and enjoy the serene atmosphere surrounding the reservoir.

•Mattupatty Factory Visit: The Mattupatty factory and learn about the local tea production process.

•Sunmoon Valley (Boating services available): Experience a tranquil boating experience amidst the serene beauty of Sunmoon Valley.

•Echo Point:Listen to your voice echo through the hills at Echo Point, a popular tourist spot.

•Tribal Village View:Get a glimpse into the unique lifestyle and culture of the local tribal community at Tribal Village View.

•Kundala Lake:Enjoy the serenity of Kundala Lake, surrounded by rolling hills and lush greenery.

•Ellapetti Vegetation View:Take in the scenic beauty and lush vegetation at Ellapetti Vegetation View.

•Topstation:Reach the highest point of Munnar at Topstation and marvel at the breathtaking panoramic views.

•Drop at Resort: Conclude the day by dropping you off at your resort, where you can unwind and relax.

Day 3

•Silent Valley National Park: Explore the pristine beauty of Silent Valley National Park, known for its rich biodiversity and untouched wilderness.

•Meesapulimala: Embark on a thrilling trek to Meesapulimala, the second-highest peak in the Western Ghats, and be rewarded with breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and valleys.

Day 4

With Kolukkumalai

•Signal View Point: Enjoy panoramic views from Signal View Point and take in the breathtaking landscapes.

•Lockhart Tea Factory Visit: Explore the Lockhart Tea Factory and learn about the tea-making process, from plucking to processing.

•Lockhart View Point: Marvel at the stunning views from Lockhart View Point and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the surroundings.

•Lockhart Gap Road: Drive along the scenic Lockhart Gap Road, surrounded by lush greenery and picturesque vistas.

•Orange Plantation: Visit an orange plantation and discover the aromatic citrus fruits grown in the region.

•Anayirankal Dam: Explore the beautiful Anayirankal Dam and enjoy the serene ambiance of the surrounding landscape.

•Hanging Bridge: Cross the Hanging Bridge, an adventurous walkway suspended over a river, and savor the thrill and scenic views.

•Powerhouse Waterfalls: Witness the majestic Powerhouse Waterfalls cascading down with great force, creating a mesmerizing spectacle.

•Return to Munnar and Drop at Resort: Conclude the day by returning to Munnar and dropping you off at your resort, where you can relax and unwind.
