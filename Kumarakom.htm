Day 1

Pick up from Munnar and drive to Kumarakom (approximately 160 km, 4-5 hours).

Check-in at a backwater resort or houseboat in Kumarakom.

Enjoy a leisurely cruise on the serene backwaters of Kumarakom. Experience the beauty of the Vembanad Lake, passing through picturesque canals and witnessing the local village life.

Visit the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, a haven for birdwatchers. Explore the sanctuary's trails and spot a variety of bird species, including migratory birds.

In the evening, enjoy a traditional Kerala-style dinner on the houseboat or at the resort.

Overnight stay on the houseboat or at the resort in Kumarakom.

Day 2

After breakfast, embark on a day tour to explore the following attractions:

Visit the Pathiramanal Island, a small scenic island located on the Vembanad Lake. Explore its natural beauty and enjoy birdwatching.

Take a visit to the Aruvikkuzhi Waterfalls, located amidst rubber plantations. Enjoy the cascades and the surrounding lush greenery.

Explore the Bay Island Driftwood Museum, which displays a unique collection of driftwood sculptures created from the remains of trees and roots found in the backwaters.

Experience a traditional Kerala Ayurvedic massage and spa treatment for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Return to the houseboat or resort and spend a peaceful evening enjoying the backwater views.

Overnight stay on the houseboat or at the resort in Kumarakom.

Day 3

After breakfast, check-out from the houseboat or resort and proceed to visit the following attractions:

Take a visit to the Kumarakom Beach, a serene and less-crowded beach where you can relax and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

Visit the Kumarakom Craft Museum, which showcases traditional crafts and artifacts representing Kerala's rich cultural heritage.

Explore the backwater canals by opting for a canoe or kayak ride. Experience the tranquility and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the surroundings.

Enjoy a traditional Kerala lunch at a local restaurant, savoring the flavors of the region's cuisine.

In the afternoon, start your return journey to Munnar.

Drop-off at the desired location in Munnar.
