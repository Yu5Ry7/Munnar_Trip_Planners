Day 1

• Attukad Waterfalls View: Marvel at the magnificent Attukad Waterfalls and enjoy the breathtaking views of cascading water amidst the lush greenery.

• Pothamedu View Point: Visit the Pothamedu View Point and witness the panoramic vistas of the tea plantations and mist-covered valleys.

• 2nd Mile View Point: Enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys from the 2nd Mile View Point.

• Ripple Waterfalls: Explore the picturesque Ripple Waterfalls and enjoy the soothing sound of the cascading water.

• Ripple Adventure Zipline: Indulge in an exhilarating adventure with ziplining activities offered by Ripple Adventure Zipline.

• Ponmudi Dam: Visit the scenic Ponmudi Dam and appreciate the serene surroundings and the view of the reservoir.

• Echo Point: Experience the echo phenomenon at Echo Point and enjoy the picturesque beauty of the surrounding hills and lake.

• Hanging Bridge: Cross the Hanging Bridge and enjoy the thrill of walking amidst nature with stunning views.

• Nadukani View Point: Witness the panoramic views of the rolling hills and mist-covered valleys from Nadukani View Point.

• Chengulam Dam: Visit Chengulam Dam and admire the tranquil waters and the surrounding scenic landscapes.

Day 2

• Idly Hills: Visit Idly Hills and enjoy the scenic beauty of the surrounding hills and valleys.

• Letchimi Estate View Point: Explore Letchimi Estate View Point and take in the breathtaking views of the tea plantations and landscapes.

• Viripara Waterfalls (Bath): Indulge in a refreshing bath at Viripara Waterfalls and revel in the natural beauty and serenity.

• Tiger Cave: Discover Tiger Cave, known for its rock formations and caves, and immerse yourself in its unique ambiance.

• Perumakoothu View Point: Visit Perumakoothu View Point and enjoy the panoramic vistas of the surrounding hills and valleys.

• Maankulom (Off-Road Separate Package): Embark on an off-road adventure to Maankulom, where you can explore the rugged terrains and enjoy thrilling experiences (requires a separate package).

• 33 Waterfalls: Marvel at the beauty of the 33 Waterfalls and witness the cascading streams amidst the lush greenery.

• Aanakulam Waterfalls: Enjoy the splendor of Aanakulam Waterfalls and revel in the tranquil atmosphere surrounding the falls.
