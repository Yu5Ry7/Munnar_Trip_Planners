Day 1

• Spices Garden Visit (Cost included in the package): Explore a spice garden and learn about the various spices grown in Munnar. Experience the aromatic flavors and scents of the region.

• Elephant Park: Get up close and personal with gentle elephants at the Elephant Park. Interact with them and even enjoy an elephant ride.

• Chengulam Dam: Visit Chengulam Dam and admire the scenic beauty of the dam surrounded by lush greenery.

• Ayurvedic Massage: Indulge in a rejuvenating Ayurvedic massage to relax and rejuvenate your mind and body.

• 2nd Mile View Point: Enjoy stunning panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys from the 2nd Mile View Point.

• Dream Land Adventure Park (Cost included in the package): Experience thrilling adventure activities at Dream Land Adventure Park, included in your package.

• Attukad Waterfalls View: Witness the mesmerizing Attukad Waterfalls and enjoy the scenic beauty of the cascading water in the midst of nature.

• Pothamedu View Point: Visit the Pothamedu View Point and savor the breathtaking views of tea plantations and mist-covered valleys.

• Zipline: Get your adrenaline pumping with an exhilarating ziplining experience in the beautiful surroundings of Munnar.

• Kathakali Show: Immerse yourself in the vibrant cultural heritage of Kerala by witnessing a captivating Kathakali performance.

Day 2

• Signal Point View: Enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys from Signal Point View.

• Flower Garden: Delight in the vibrant colors and fragrances of beautiful flowers at the Flower Garden.

• Photo Point: Capture memorable moments amidst the picturesque settings of Photo Point.

• Elephant Park: Interact with gentle elephants and witness their majestic presence at the Elephant Park.

• Botanical Garden: Explore the lush greenery and diverse flora at the Botanical Garden.

• Mattupetty Dam: Visit the scenic Mattupetty Dam and enjoy boating services available at Sunmoon Valley.

• Echo Point: Experience the fascinating echo phenomenon at Echo Point and enjoy the serene surroundings.

• Tribal Village View: Get a glimpse into the indigenous tribal culture and lifestyle at the Tribal Village View.

• Kundala Lake: Enjoy the tranquil ambiance and scenic beauty of Kundala Lake surrounded by rolling hills.

• Ellapetti Vegetation View: Witness the lush green vegetation and captivating landscapes at Ellapetti.

• Top Station: Visit the famous Top Station and enjoy panoramic views of the Western Ghats and neighboring Tamil Nadu.

Day 3

• Lockhart View Point: Enjoy scenic views of the Lockhart Gap and surrounding mountains from Lockhart View Point.

• Tea Museum: Explore the Tea Museum and learn about the history and production of tea in Munnar.

• Eravikulam National Park: Discover the rich biodiversity of Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr and lush landscapes.

• Thalayaar View Point: Enjoy panoramic views of the majestic Thalayaar Waterfalls and the surrounding natural beauty.

• Lakkom Waterfall: Visit Lakkom Waterfall and admire the cascading beauty of this pristine natural wonder.
