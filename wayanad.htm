Day 1

Pick up from Munnar and drive to Wayanad (approximately 230 km, 6-7 hours).

Check-in at a hotel or resort in Wayanad.

Start your exploration with a visit to the Edakkal Caves, known for its ancient rock carvings and panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

Afterward, head to the Soochipara Waterfalls, where you can enjoy a refreshing dip in the natural pool and admire the picturesque cascades.

Visit the Pookode Lake, a serene freshwater lake surrounded by lush greenery. You can go for a boat ride or simply enjoy a leisurely walk around the lake.

Return to the hotel and relax for the evening.

Overnight stay in Wayanad.

Day 2

After breakfast, visit the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary. Embark on a thrilling safari to spot wildlife such as elephants, deer, monkeys, and various bird species.

Explore the enchanting Muthanga Wildlife Sanctuary, known for its rich biodiversity and lush forests. Enjoy a guided trekking or jeep safari experience.

Visit the Banasura Sagar Dam, the largest earthen dam in India. Enjoy the scenic beauty, go for a boat ride, or opt for a trek to the nearby hills.

If time permits, you can also visit the Thirunelli Temple, an ancient Hindu temple surrounded by the Brahmagiri Hills. Seek blessings and soak in the spiritual atmosphere.

Return to the hotel and relax for the evening.

Overnight stay in Wayanad

Day 3

After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and proceed to explore the following attractions:

Visit the picturesque Meenmutty Waterfalls, known for its three-tiered cascades and scenic surroundings. Enjoy a trek through the dense forests to reach the falls.

Explore the Wayanad Heritage Museum, which showcases a collection of artifacts, tribal relics, and historical items related to the region's culture and heritage.

Visit the Lakkidi Viewpoint, which offers breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys and winding roads.

In the afternoon, you can visit the Kuruvadweep, a cluster of islands formed by the Kabini River. Explore the dense forests, enjoy a boat ride, and spot various flora and fauna.

In the evening, start your return journey to Munnar.

Drop-off at the desired location in Munnar.
