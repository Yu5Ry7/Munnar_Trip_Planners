Day 1

• Idly Hills: Visit Idly Hills and enjoy the scenic beauty of the surrounding hills and valleys.

• Letchimi Estate View Point: Explore Letchimi Estate View Point and take in the breathtaking views of the tea plantations and landscapes.

• Viripara Waterfalls (Bath): Indulge in a refreshing bath at Viripara Waterfalls and revel in the natural beauty and serenity.

• Tiger Cave: Discover Tiger Cave, known for its rock formations and caves, and immerse yourself in its unique ambiance.

• Perumakoothu View Point: Visit Perumakoothu View Point and enjoy the panoramic vistas of the surrounding hills and valleys.

• Maankulom (Off-Road Separate Package): Embark on an off-road adventure to Maankulom, where you can explore the rugged terrains and enjoy thrilling experiences (requires a separate package).

• 33 Waterfalls: Marvel at the beauty of the 33 Waterfalls and witness the cascading streams amidst the lush greenery.

• Aanakulam Waterfalls: Enjoy the splendor of Aanakulam Waterfalls and revel in the tranquil atmosphere surrounding the falls.

• Spices Garden Visit : Explore a spice garden and learn about the various spices grown in Munnar. Experience the aromatic flavors and scents of the region.

• Elephant Park: Get up close and personal with gentle elephants at the Elephant Park. Interact with them and even enjoy an elephant ride.

• Chengulam Dam: Visit Chengulam Dam and admire the scenic beauty of the dam surrounded by lush greenery.

• Ayurvedic Massage: Indulge in a rejuvenating Ayurvedic massage to relax and rejuvenate your mind and body.

• 2nd Mile View Point: Enjoy stunning panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys from the 2nd Mile View Point.

• Dream Land Adventure Park : Experience thrilling adventure activities at Dream Land Adventure Park, included in your package.

• Attukad Waterfalls View: Witness the mesmerizing Attukad Waterfalls and enjoy the scenic beauty of the cascading water in the midst of nature.

• Pothamedu View Point: Visit the Pothamedu View Point and savor the breathtaking views of tea plantations and mist-covered valleys.

• Zipline: Get your adrenaline pumping with an exhilarating ziplining experience in the beautiful surroundings of Munnar.

• Kathakali Show: Immerse yourself in the vibrant cultural heritage of Kerala by witnessing a captivating Kathakali performance.
