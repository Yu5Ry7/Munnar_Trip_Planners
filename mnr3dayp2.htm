Day 1

With Kolukkumalai

•Signal View Point: Enjoy panoramic views from Signal View Point and take in the breathtaking landscapes.

•Lockhart Tea Factory Visit: Explore the Lockhart Tea Factory and learn about the tea-making process, from plucking to processing.

•Lockhart View Point: Marvel at the stunning views from Lockhart View Point and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the surroundings.

•Lockhart Gap Road: Drive along the scenic Lockhart Gap Road, surrounded by lush greenery and picturesque vistas.

•Orange Plantation: Visit an orange plantation and discover the aromatic citrus fruits grown in the region.

•Anayirankal Dam: Explore the beautiful Anayirankal Dam and enjoy the serene ambiance of the surrounding landscape.

•Hanging Bridge: Cross the Hanging Bridge, an adventurous walkway suspended over a river, and savor the thrill and scenic views.

•Powerhouse Waterfalls: Witness the majestic Powerhouse Waterfalls cascading down with great force, creating a mesmerizing spectacle.

•Return to Munnar and Drop at Resort: Conclude the day by returning to Munnar and dropping you off at your resort, where you can relax and unwind.

Day 2

• Thalayaar View Point: Enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys from Thalayaar View Point.

• Lakkom Waterfall: Marvel at the natural beauty of Lakkom Waterfall, known for its cascading streams and lush greenery.

• Marayoor Sandalwood Reserve: Visit the Marayoor Sandalwood Reserve and learn about the cultivation and significance of sandalwood.

• Muniara: Explore the Muniara region, known for its serene ambiance and scenic landscapes.

• Kanthaloor Apple Farm: Experience the charm of Kanthaloor Apple Farm and witness the picturesque apple orchards in bloom.

• Erachillpara Waterfalls (Seasonal): If the season permits, visit the Erachillpara Waterfalls and enjoy the natural beauty and tranquility.

• Checkdam (Seasonal): If accessible, visit the Checkdam and appreciate the surrounding natural landscape.

• Shooting Point: Capture stunning photographs and enjoy the scenic views from the Shooting Point.

• Strawberry Farm: Visit a strawberry farm and indulge in the delightful experience of picking fresh strawberries.

• Honeyrock: Discover Honeyrock, known for its honey production and learn about the fascinating world of bees.

• Jaggery Factory: Visit a jaggery factory and witness the traditional process of making jaggery from sugarcane.

Day 3

• Attukad Waterfalls View: Marvel at the mesmerizing Attukad Waterfalls and soak in the beauty of the cascading water amidst lush green surroundings.

• Pothamedu View Point: Enjoy panoramic views of the tea plantations and mist-covered valleys from Pothamedu View Point.

• 2nd Mile View Point: Capture stunning vistas of the surrounding hills and valleys from the scenic 2nd Mile View Point.

• Ripple Waterfalls: Discover the serene and picturesque Ripple Waterfalls, where you can relax and enjoy the soothing sound of cascading water.

• Ripple Adventure Zipline: Experience an adrenaline rush as you zip through the lush landscapes and enjoy an exciting ziplining adventure.

• Ponmudi Dam: Visit the Ponmudi Dam and admire the tranquil reservoir amidst the scenic beauty of nature.

• Echo Point: Enjoy the natural echo phenomenon at Echo Point, where your voice resonates and reverberates amidst the beautiful surroundings.

• Hanging Bridge: Cross the hanging bridge and marvel at the breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape and gushing streams.

• Nadukani View Point: Revel in the panoramic vistas of lush hills, valleys, and tea plantations from Nadukani View Point.

• Chengulam Dam: Visit Chengulam Dam and appreciate the tranquility of the reservoir against the backdrop of picturesque scenery.
