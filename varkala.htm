Day 1

Pick up from Munnar and drive to Varkala (approximately 290 km, 7-8 hours).

Check-in at a hotel or resort in Varkala.

Spend the day relaxing at the beautiful Varkala Beach. Enjoy the golden sands, take a dip in the Arabian Sea, and witness the stunning sunset.

Explore the Varkala Cliff, a popular cliffside area with shops, restaurants, and cafes offering a panoramic view of the beach.

Visit the Janardanaswamy Temple, a famous Hindu temple located near the beach. Admire the intricate architecture and seek blessings.

Overnight stay in Varkala.

Day 2

After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and proceed to explore the following attractions:

Visit the Varkala Aquarium, which showcases a variety of marine life and provides insights into the underwater world.

Take a short drive to the nearby Papanasam Beach and enjoy a tranquil beach experience away from the bustling tourist crowds.

In the afternoon, you can visit the Varkala Lighthouse, located on a cliff offering panoramic views of the coastline. Climb to the top for a breathtaking view.

If time permits, you can also explore the nearby Edava Beach or venture further to explore the backwaters of Alleppey.

In the evening, start your return journey to Munnar.

Drop-off at the desired location in Munnar.
