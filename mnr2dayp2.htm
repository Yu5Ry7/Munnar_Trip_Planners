Day 1

• Lockhart View Point: Enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys and tea plantations from Lockhart View Point.

• Tea Museum: Explore the Tea Museum and learn about the history and process of tea production in Munnar.

• Eravikulam National Park: Discover the rich biodiversity of Eravikulam National Park and spot endangered Nilgiri Tahr.

• Thalayaar View Point: Marvel at the majestic Thalayaar Waterfalls and soak in the beauty of the cascading waters.

• Lakkom Waterfall: Visit Lakkom Waterfall and enjoy the serene atmosphere and the sight of water gushing down the rocks.

• Drop at Munnar: Conclude the day by dropping you off at your accommodation in Munnar, where you can relax and unwind.

Day 2

• Silent Valley Sitescene: Explore the picturesque beauty of Silent Valley and immerse yourself in the tranquil surroundings.

• Meesapulimala: Embark on an adventurous trek to Meesapulimala, the second-highest peak in the Western Ghats, and witness stunning panoramic views.
