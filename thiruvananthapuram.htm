Day 1

Pick up from Munnar and drive to Thiruvananthapuram (approximately 300 km, 7-8 hours).

Check-in at a hotel in Thiruvananthapuram.

Visit the iconic Padmanabhaswamy Temple, known for its stunning Dravidian architecture and religious significance.

Explore the Napier Museum, which houses a diverse collection of historical artifacts and artwork.

Take a stroll along the Kovalam Beach, a popular tourist destination known for its golden sands and calm waters.

Enjoy a sunset view at the Veli Tourist Village, a scenic waterfront area with a beautiful lagoon and garden.

Overnight stay at a hotel in Thiruvananthapuram.

Day 2

After breakfast, embark on a day tour to explore the following attractions:

Visit the Kuthira Malika (Puthen Malika) Palace, a splendid palace known for its exquisite wooden carvings and architecture.

Explore the Kanakakkunnu Palace and its surrounding gardens, offering a glimpse into Kerala's royal history.

Discover the rich biodiversity at the Thiruvananthapuram Zoo, home to various animal species, including lions, tigers, and elephants.

Explore the Shanghumukham Beach and enjoy activities like swimming, beach volleyball, or simply relaxing by the sea.

Visit the Attukal Bhagavathy Temple, one of the prominent temples in Kerala dedicated to the Hindu goddess Bhagavathy.

Return to the hotel and spend the evening at leisure.

Overnight stay at a hotel in Thiruvananthapuram.

Day 3

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to visit the following attractions:

Take a visit to the iconic Sri Chitra Art Gallery, showcasing an extensive collection of Indian art, including paintings, sculptures, and traditional artifacts.

Explore the Neyyar Wildlife Sanctuary, a haven for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts, offering opportunities for boating and wildlife spotting.

Visit the Poovar Island, a scenic island located at the mouth of the Neyyar River, known for its pristine beaches and backwater cruises.

Enjoy shopping for traditional handicrafts, spices, and souvenirs at the local markets, such as Chalai Market and Connemara Market.

In the evening, start your return journey to Munnar.

Drop-off at the desired location in Munnar.
