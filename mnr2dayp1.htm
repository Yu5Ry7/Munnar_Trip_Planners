Day 1

• Spices Garden Visit (Cost included in the package): Explore the aromatic Spice Garden and learn about various spices and their cultivation.

• Elephant Park: Interact with majestic elephants and enjoy an up-close encounter with these gentle giants.

• Chengulam Dam: Visit the scenic Chengulam Dam and soak in the picturesque surroundings.

• Ayurvedic Massage: Indulge in a relaxing Ayurvedic massage to rejuvenate your body and mind.

• 2nd Mile View Point: Witness breathtaking panoramic views of the valleys and tea plantations at the 2nd Mile View Point.

• Dream Land Adventure Park (Cost included in the package): Experience thrilling rides and exciting activities at the Dream Land Adventure Park.

• Attukad Waterfalls View: Marvel at the captivating beauty of Attukad Waterfalls amidst lush greenery.

• Pothamedu View Point: Visit the scenic Pothamedu View Point and enjoy stunning vistas of tea and coffee plantations.

• Zipline: Feel the adrenaline rush as you soar through the treetops on a thrilling zipline adventure.

• Kathakali Show: Witness the mesmerizing traditional dance form of Kerala, Kathakali, showcasing vibrant costumes and expressive performances.

• Drop at Resort/Cottage in Munnar: Conclude the day by returning to your resort/cottage in Munnar, where you can relax and unwind.

Day 2

• Signal Point View: Enjoy the panoramic views from Signal Point and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the surroundings.

• Flower Garden: Explore the vibrant Flower Garden and marvel at the colorful blooms and intricate designs.

• Photo Point: Capture beautiful memories at Photo Point, known for its picturesque backdrops and stunning landscapes.

• Elephant Park: Interact with majestic elephants and witness their incredible presence at the Elephant Park.

• Botanical Garden: Discover the rich flora and fauna at the Botanical Garden, home to a diverse range of plant species.

• Mattupatty Dam: Visit the scenic Mattupatty Dam and enjoy the serene atmosphere surrounding the reservoir.

• Mattupatty Factory Visit: Explore the Mattupatty factory and learn about the local tea production process.

• Sunmoon Valley (Boating services available): Experience a tranquil boating experience amidst the serene beauty of Sunmoon Valley.

• Echo Point: Listen to your voice echo through the hills at Echo Point, a popular tourist spot.

• Tribal Village View: Get a glimpse into the unique lifestyle and culture of the local tribal community at Tribal Village View.

• Kundala Lake: Enjoy the serenity of Kundala Lake, surrounded by rolling hills and lush greenery.

• Ellapetti Vegetation View: Take in the scenic beauty and lush vegetation at Ellapetti Vegetation View.

• Top Station: Visit Top Station, the highest point in Munnar, and enjoy panoramic views of the Western Ghats.

• Drop at Resort: Conclude your day by returning to your resort in Munnar and unwind in the tranquil surroundings.
