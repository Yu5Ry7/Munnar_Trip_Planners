Day 1

Pick up from Munnar and drive to Thekkady (approximately 90 km, 3 hours).

Check-in at a hotel or resort in Thekkady.

In the afternoon, visit to Periyar National Park and a boat safari on Periyar Lake to spot wildlife, especially elephants and birds.

Will explore the spice plantations in the area and learn about the various spices grown in Thekkady.

In the evening, enjoy a cultural show featuring traditional music and dance forms of Kerala. Overnight stay in Thekkady.

Day 2

After breakfast, go for a trek in the beautiful Periyar Tiger Reserve. You can choose from various trekking trails available, ranging from easy to moderate levels.

Visit the Mangala Devi Temple, located within the Periyar Tiger Reserve, which offers panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys.

Explore the local market in Thekkady and shop for spices, handicrafts, and souvenirs. In the evening, you can opt for an Ayurvedic massage or relax at a nearby spa.

Overnight stay in Thekkady.

Day 3

After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and proceed to visit the nearby attractions:

Visit the Elephant Junction,where you can enjoy elephant rides, elephant bathing, and interact with these gentle giants.

Explore the nearby hill station of Kumily and visit the Kumily Market, known for its fresh spices and local produce.

In the afternoon, start your return journey to Munnar.

Drop-off at the desired location in Munnar.
