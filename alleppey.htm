Day 1

Pick up from Munnar and drive to Alleppey (approximately 170 km, 4-5 hours).

Check-in at a houseboat or resort in Alleppey, known for its backwaters and scenic beauty.

Embark on a memorable houseboat cruise through the famous backwaters of Alleppey. Enjoy the serene surroundings, lush green landscapes, and observe the local way of life.
Relax and unwind on the houseboat while enjoying traditional Kerala cuisine prepared by the onboard chef.

Cruise along the backwaters, passing through narrow canals, paddy fields, and charming villages.

Overnight stay on the houseboat.

Day 2

After breakfast on the houseboat, disembark and proceed to explore Alleppey further.

Visit the beautiful Alleppey Beach, known for its golden sands, azure waters, and vibrant local life. Spend some time relaxing by the beach and enjoying the coastal atmosphere.

Explore the famous Alleppey Backwaters by taking a traditional Kerala-style canoe ride. Experience the tranquility of the backwaters up close and witness the unique ecosystem.

Visit the Alleppey Lighthouse for panoramic views of the city and the surrounding backwaters.

In the evening, you can opt for a relaxing Ayurvedic massage and rejuvenation session to experience the traditional wellness practices of Kerala.

Overnight stay in Alleppey

Day 3

After breakfast, check-out from the hotel or resort and visit the nearby attractions:

Explore the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, located on the banks of Vembanad Lake. Spot various species of migratory birds and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

Take a boat ride on Vembanad Lake, the largest freshwater lake in Kerala, and enjoy the scenic beauty and backwater views.

Visit the Pathiramanal Island, a small enchanting island in the lake, known for its rich birdlife and picturesque landscapes.

In the afternoon, start your return journey to Munnar.

Drop-off at the desired location in Munnar.
